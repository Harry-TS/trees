City Trees

by Steve Merel


Walk up to a tree, or tap it on the map, to identify it.

Tap the button in the lower right corner of the map to center it on your location, and again to also orient the map to your current heading.

Tree data comes from databases made publicly available by each city, and typically contains trees on city land, such as parks, sidewalks, and median strips.

Don't see data for your city? Email me and I might be able to add it.
